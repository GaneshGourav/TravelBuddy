Travelling Within the Maldives

Since the islands are tiny with narrow roads, driving to get from one place to another is out of the question. You can walk or cycle across the tinier islands, whereas larger islands such as Malé have taxi services. The most excitement during Maldives holidays however, is found during island hopping within the Maldives. Based on your budget you can take a leisurely cruise in a traditional Maldivian 'Dhoni', beat the waves in a speedboat, a ferry or even take a yacht with a full bar and cabins, or you could fly between the islands in a seaplane.
